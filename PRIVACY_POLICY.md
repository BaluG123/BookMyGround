# Privacy Policy for BookMyGround

**Last Updated: April 15, 2026**

Welcome to **BookMyGround**. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and our website (https://bookmyground.pythonanywhere.com).

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Personal Information
When you register for an account or make a booking, we may collect:
*   **Name** and contact details (Email, Phone Number).
*   **Payout Details**: For ground owners, we collect UPI IDs and bank account names to facilitate payments.

### B. Transaction Information
When you book a ground, we collect details about the transaction, including the ground name, date, time, and amount. **Payment processing is handled securely via Razorpay.** We do not store your full credit card or bank login credentials on our servers.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:
*   Facilitate and confirm your turf bookings.
*   Process payments and platform commissions.
*   Send booking confirmations and status updates.
*   Prevent fraudulent transactions and ensure platform security.
*   Provide customer support.

---

## 3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information in the following cases:
*   **With Ground Owners**: We share the customer's name and phone number with the ground owner to verify the booking at the venue.
*   **With Service Providers**: We share data with Razorpay to process your payments securely.
*   **Legal Requirements**: If required by law or to protect our legal rights.

---

## 4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security protocols to protect your data. All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS.

---

## 5. Your Rights

You have the right to:
*   Access the personal information we hold about you.
*   Request the correction of inaccurate data.
*   Request the deletion of your account and personal data.

---

## 6. Refund & Cancellation Policy

Bookings can be cancelled up to 24 hours before the scheduled slot for a partial refund. Platform commissions (29 INR) are non-refundable. For more details, please see our full Refund Policy on our website.
